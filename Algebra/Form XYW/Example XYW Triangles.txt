
Examples

- Form XYW (x..[y]..w). And points: P(-x, x*y), P(w, w*y).
- From a form xyw(x,y,w) we obtain a right triangle with rational sides and area equal to n: TRQ(y, 2n/y, (x+w)/y).
If two forms XYW arise from the same form PQRS the triangles are equal and the sides are integers.
- Also n*(D^2) will be in the form PQRS.
