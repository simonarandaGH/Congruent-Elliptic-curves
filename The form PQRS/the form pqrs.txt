# The form-pqrs

Any integer that is: n= p*q = r*s, with s= p+q+r. 
All numbers being integers.

Each pqrs-form gives:

- 2 points on the elliptic curve.
    (-p*r, p*r*(q+r) ), (-q*r, y= q*r*(p+r)).
    In both cases: x=x/D^2;y=y/D^3;

- 1 RTZ right triangle with integer sides.
    RTZ (p+r, q+r, p+q).

- 1 triad of equidistant N squares: (s1/2, s2/2, s3/2).

Properties: 
  q* (p-r)= r* (p+r), 
  ps= qr + (p+r)^2, qs= pr + (q+r)^2, 
  n^2 = p* q* r* s.
